Left: About the School

Tokyo Jujutsu High serves not only as a training ground for the next generation of sorcerers but a headquarters for all alumni who have graduated on to be full-fledged jujutsu sorcerers as well. The current principal is Masamichi Yaga.

Tokyo Jujutsu High is a broad campus of several buildings build in a traditional Japanese architectural style, operating under the guise of a Buddhist temple. The school building is well hidden on Tokyo's outskirts, far and high in the mountains.

The uniforms are highly customizable, and you can have your own variations of the uniform coz we respect fashion.

Meet Your Classmates!

Megumi Fushiguro

Megumi is a very outwardly stoic, aloof, and calculating individual. He usually has a neutral facial expression that he rarely changes and appears to take every situation seriously.

Technique: Ten Shadows Technique
Innate Domain: Chimera Shadow Garden

Nobara Kugisaki

Nobara is a confident and brash young woman with an unshakable character. More than anything, Nobara is determined to stay true to herself no matter what. She takes great pride in being both a pretty girl and a strong fighter, refusing to let anyone influence her.

Techniques: Stray Doll Technique and Resonance

Yuji Itadori

Yuji is a passionate, good-natured and friendly person. He highly commends "the value of life," and to this end, he will ensure that others receive a "proper death". If you need someone to hangout with and have fun, then he is your person.

A word of warning - If you notice black colored lines forming on Yuji's body, RUN FOR YOUR LIFE!
